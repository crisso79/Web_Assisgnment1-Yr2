Gordon is the co-founder and Managing Director of GVD, responsible for driving the strategic development of the company. Gordon has nearly 20 years’ experience within the reseller/dealer sector, having previously worked with Irish internet service providers Indigo and Eir (Eircom).
Gordon’s extensive knowledge of IP and wireless technologies means that he has real insight into the needs of resellers and installers. His focus on technology innovation and customer service has been key to the success and reputation of GVD.
As Technical Director of GVD Owen is head of Technical Support, Product R&D and develops our training programmes. With 20 years in engineering and IT, Owen’s key strength lies in making technology accessible for all.
Director and shareholder of GVD, Rory has been with the company for 10 years and is responsible for the management of our channel of systems integrators in both Ireland and the UK. Rory is responsible for driving sales across our channel of integrators and engaging with specifiers to ensure our products are specified for large capital projects across all verticals. Through the channel, Rory has been involved in some of the largest IP security projects in both Ireland and the UK.
