Who we Are

GVD is a value-added distributor of IP technology and drone solutions. We offer consultancy, design and delivery on projects of all sizes, in Ireland and the UK.
Founded by Gordon Smith in 2003, GVD is at the forefront of the move from analogue to IP-based security systems.
We help system integrators and consulting engineers to specify, design, and test the perfect surveillance solutions for their clients.

What we Do


We offer a wide range of services that help you get the most out of IP technology and solutions. We also offer outstanding pre-sales and post-sales support.
